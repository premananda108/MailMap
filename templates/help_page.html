Frequently Asked Questions

What if my photo doesn't have GPS data?

You can manually specify coordinates in the email subject line using the format: lat:40.7128,lng:-74.0060. Registered users can also add photos directly on the map and set the location.

How long does it take for my story to appear?

Stories typically appear on the map within seconds of sending your email. You'll receive a confirmation email with a direct link to your story.

Can I edit or delete my story after posting?

Registered users can edit or delete their own stories directly on the map. For stories submitted via email without registration, if you need to remove content, please contact the moderation team through the reporting system.

What image formats are supported?

Most common image formats are supported including JPEG, PNG, and GIF. JPEG images typically contain the best GPS data.

Is there a limit to story length or image size?

Keep stories concise and engaging. Images should be under 10MB for optimal processing speed.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Each story on the map has reporting options. The community-driven moderation system helps maintain content quality.
